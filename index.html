I was bored, so I built a functioning brain in Minecraft

This is a fully functioning image recognition nueral network modeled in Minecraft. It's based on how our own brains recognize images. It will "read" what's on the input (retina) and make a guess as to what digit is written. For the most part, it will work with just about any numerical digit that you will visually recognize.

Training Mr. Squiggles to take over the world.

Here we are training 4 copies of the same AI bot named Mr. Squiggles. Mr. Squiggles doesn't know anything and isn't programmed to do anything. The only difference between each screen is the environment and the goal on which each bot's "fitness" rating is scored:

  • Top-Left: Distance run to the right (running)
  • Top-Right: Vertical distance clearned (Jumping)
  • Bottom-Left: Obstactle avoidance
  • Bottom-Right: Climbing stairs

In each iteration, 25 Mr. Squiggles bots are generated at the same time. The ones that happen to perform tasks best get to breed. Each generation uses the natural process of evolution to train the bots on their respoective tasks. Feel free to skip ahead to see how they all work out.
